A little about me

I'm always looking to expand my skillset and my portfolio, so if there is anything that you need done on the web, let me know

I have been developing websites for 12 years, seeing the many changes and evolution of how web development has advanced over the years. I started developing for a job as a freelancer while I was working swing-shift at a glass factory, then development became my passion. Beginning with making Myspace sites I have learned more about web developing as the newest trends emerge including MVC frameworks, version control and building web apps. I am constantly learning new ways to use the languages that I know to enable clients to do more with the tools that I build.
